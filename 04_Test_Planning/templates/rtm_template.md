## Mapping Guide
- **Req ID:** Trace back to Jira Story or Product Specification Document.
- **Test Case ID:** Link to the exact test case scripts (e.g., `TC_LOGIN_001`).
- **Test Status:** Passed, Failed, Blocked, or Not Executed.
- **Defect ID:** Track open bug cards (e.g., `BUG_102`) linked to the failed scenario.

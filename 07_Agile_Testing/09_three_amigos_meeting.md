# The Three Amigos Meeting

## What is a Three Amigos Meeting?
The **Three Amigos** meeting is a core Agile ceremony where the three main perspectives of software development collaborate before any coding starts:

```
               👔 [ Business Perspective ] (Product Owner / BA)
                                 │
                                 ├─────── Shared Understanding
                                 │
         👨‍💻 [ Technology ] ──────┼────── 🧪 [ Quality Assurance ]
            (Developer)          │             (QA Engineer)
```

- **The Product Owner (Business):** What problem are we trying to solve? What is the business value?
- **The Developer (Technology):** How will we build this technically? What are the architectural boundaries?
- **The QA Engineer (Quality):** What could go wrong? What edge cases, negative flows, or security checks are needed?

---

## When Does it Happen?
- **Timeline:** During Backlog Refinement or early in Sprint Planning.
- **Duration:** 10-15 minutes per user story.
- **Focus:** Discussing requirements, refining user acceptance criteria, and ensuring the story meets the **Definition of Ready (DoR)**.

---

## 📌 Key Takeaways
- Prevents development from starting on stories that are ambiguous or incomplete.
- Encourages shared ownership of quality—it is no longer just the "QA's job".
- Aligns all three views on the exact definition of "Done".

## 📖 What Is This Repository?

This is a **free, open-source, community-driven** software testing knowledge base that covers **everything** a QA engineer needs — from day one basics to expert-level automation, security, and performance testing.

Whether you are:
- 🎓 A **student** starting your QA career
- 💼 A **Manual Tester** wanting to grow into automation
- 🤖 An **Automation Engineer** looking for best practices
- 🧑‍💼 A **QA Lead/Manager** who needs templates & frameworks
- 🎯 A **Developer** who wants to write better tests

**This repository is for you.**
